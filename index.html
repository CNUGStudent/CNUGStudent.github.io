Who We Are

CNUGStudent Stands for Chennai .NET User Group (Student Chapter) and is supported by Microsoft Corporation through the initiatives of the Microsoft Student Partners, Chennai (MSP – CHN) who form the backbone of the group. The Microsoft Student Partners (MSPs) are a select set of students from different walks and spheres of academia guided by a common set of characteristics such as passion for technology, initiative and a never ending, never ceasing thirst for knowledge.

Video

What We Do

Driven by the initiatives of the MSPs and the support of students and technology enthusiasts throughout the region, CNUGStudent aims to spread knowledge and information on various technology domains though different student-centric programs such as workshops and seminars for different colleges, city-wide events such as the Microsoft Academic Developer Conference (AcadDevCon), online activities and competitions and various other means.
